About Us

A health company is considering to launch its own health tracking app which could be connected to customer’s smart gadgets and be a one-stop platform for all trackable health data. The company has a reputation of producing eco-friendly packaging for their products, which by themselves are all-natural and organically made. It aims to minimize environment destructive material use such as plastics, paper and glass type. They aspire to share this message visually through their app as well while still keeping the primary function of the app to be health data tracking service. Your job is to design the landing page for this company’s health tracking app using minimal features yet giving it a modern and classic look. You may choose the theme, the lay, the structure as you like.
